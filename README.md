# ClaimIntel

> **Smarter Claims Analysis. Better Decisions.**

ClaimIntel is an AI-powered claims intelligence platform designed to help insurance professionals review, evaluate, and manage complex liability claims more efficiently. Rather than simply summarizing claim files, ClaimIntel analyzes documentation, identifies key risk factors, highlights missing information, and generates structured recommendations to support better claim decisions.

The platform is designed for claims professionals handling Commercial Auto, General Liability, Personal Auto, Property, and other casualty claims, providing actionable insights throughout the claim lifecycle.

---

## Overview

ClaimIntel combines artificial intelligence with structured claims handling practices to assist adjusters, supervisors, TPAs, carriers, and risk managers in evaluating claim exposure, organizing evidence, and preparing for negotiations or litigation.

The goal is not to replace professional judgment—it is to reduce manual review time while improving consistency, documentation quality, and decision support.

---

## Current Features

### AI Claim Review

* Analyze uploaded claim documentation
* Generate structured claim summaries
* Identify important facts and timelines
* Organize complex information into an easy-to-read format

### Liability Analysis

* Evaluate liability strengths and weaknesses
* Highlight disputed facts
* Assess comparative negligence considerations
* Identify additional investigation opportunities

### Coverage Review

* Summarize available coverage information
* Identify potential coverage issues
* Highlight policy considerations requiring further review

### Venue Intelligence

* Review jurisdiction-specific litigation considerations
* Identify venue characteristics that may impact claim exposure
* Support litigation planning

### Medical & Damages Analysis

* Summarize injuries and treatment chronology
* Organize medical timelines
* Identify treatment gaps
* Highlight significant damages and future exposure

### Claim Readiness Assessment

* Identify missing documentation
* Highlight incomplete investigations
* Recommend additional evidence collection
* Evaluate overall claim readiness

### Follow-Up Assistant

* Ask additional questions after the report is generated
* Receive contextual responses based on uploaded claim information
* Continue claim analysis without restarting the review

### Professional Report Generation

Generate structured reports suitable for:

* Claim file documentation
* Supervisor reviews
* Reserve discussions
* Mediation preparation
* Litigation strategy meetings

# Vision

ClaimIntel is being developed as an AI-powered decision support platform for insurance claims professionals.

The objective is to assist, not replace, experienced adjusters by providing structured analysis, surfacing key information, reducing repetitive administrative work, and supporting informed decision-making throughout the claims process.

---

# Disclaimer

ClaimIntel is intended as a decision support tool only. It does not provide legal advice, insurance coverage opinions, or claim settlement authority. Final claim decisions remain the responsibility of licensed claims professionals and their organizations.

Users should only upload claim information they are authorized to access and handle in accordance with applicable company policies, privacy requirements, and regulatory obligations.
